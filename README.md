## Tackle
**Tackle** is a gamified mobile productivity app designed to help students overcome distractions and build healthy study habits. By centralising task management, time tracking, and distraction blocking into one platform, Tackle turns productivity into an engaging experience through a customiable avatar and social features.

---

### 🚀 Key Features
#### 🛠️ Productivity Tools

- **Task Manager:** Create and organize tasks with specific deadlines, priority levels, and custom colors.
- **Integrated Timer:** Track focus sessions in real-time with an animated progress bar.
- **Distraction Blocker:** Discourages users from leaving the app during active sessions to maintain focus integrity
- **Statistics:** View weekly progress via a dynamic bar chart tracking total focus duration.

#### 🎮 Gamification
- **Customisable Avatar:** Meet Tack, a personal companion built with layered sprite sheets for smooth animation.
- **Reward System:** Earn coins based on the duration of focus sessions, featuring a 20% bonus for full completion.
- **Store & Inventory:** Use earned coins to purchase and equip digital cosmetics that refresh daily.

#### 👥 Social & Collaboration 
- **Friend System:** Find and add friends using secure, unique 6-character Friend Codes.
- **Group Tasks:** Collaborate with up to four friends on shared goals with real-time progress syncing.
- **Activity Feed:** Share session completions, like friends' posts, and foster community accountability.
